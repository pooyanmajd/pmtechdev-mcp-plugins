## Safety boundaries

Read-only mode is the default. Draft and state-change capabilities are optional and carry additional risk. Configuration gates reduce accidental actions but do not replace human judgment. Mailbridge v0.1 has no send tool. Email content is untrusted and may contain misleading or malicious instructions.

Mailbridge is not an archival, backup, legal-discovery, emergency communication, compliance, or delivery-guarantee service. Verify important actions directly in Mail.app and with the intended recipient when appropriate.
